Starry Adventure: Astronomical Journey in the Vicinity of LA

Jia Kim
°
Falling Stars
°
05 Feb, 2024

Hello, fellow travelers! Today, let's embark on a dreamy and incomparably beautiful journey together—a journey where we listen to the songs of the stars and open our eyes to the infinite beauty of the cosmos. Our destinations? Joshua Tree National Park, Griffith Observatory, and the Grand Canyon.

1. First Impressions at Joshua Tree National Park

Our first stop is Joshua Tree National Park, stretching across the border of California and Nevada, renowned for its picturesque sunsets and mesmerizing stars. Here, you can experience the unique Joshua Trees against the backdrop of the vast universe. At night, the silent embrace of nature, combined with the unfolding stage of the stars, feels like a celestial guide leading us into the cosmos.

Rock Formations and Joshua Trees on the Drive

Upon entering Joshua Tree National Park, you'll find well-maintained roads stretching endlessly. Enjoy a road trip filled with the unique Joshua Trees, various shrubs, and magnificent rock formations created by nature.

Adorable Cacti Warnings!

During the day, while waiting for the stars, take the time to capture photos of the diverse and cute cacti unique to Joshua Tree National Park. However, be cautious as some of these seemingly adorable cacti carry lethal toxins.Watch your steps and handle them with care when capturing their beauty through your camera lens.

Nature's Art: Skull Rock

Among the many rocks in Joshua Tree National Park, none are as distinctive as Skull Rock. The combination of its massive size and the skull-like shape crafted by nature captures the imagination.

Camping under the Stars

Joshua Tree National Park is renowned for its camping sites. Camping under the night sky, amidst the chilly desert air, accompanied by warm food, creates unforgettable memories.

2. Communicating with the Stars at Griffith Observatory

Next, our journey takes us to Griffith Observatory, an iconic symbol in the western United States, located in Griffith Park, Los Angeles. This observatory is one of the most famous astronomical research facilities in the country. Utilizing telescopes, we begin our communication with the mysteries of the universe. Especially at night, away from the city lights, encountering the smiles of the stars brings a serene peace to the heart.

Hollywood Sign Views

Griffith Observatory offers a perfect vantage point for capturing photos with the iconic Hollywood sign. The lush forest and the sunset's reddish hues become even more enchanting here.

Captivating Night Sky

If you haven't seen the stars from the observatory, the city lights below create a mesmerizing backdrop. The night skyline of LA, seen from Griffith Observatory, is truly breathtaking.

3. Into the Deep Cosmos of the Grand Canyon

Lastly, our journey leads us to the Grand Canyon. Marvel at its breathtaking views during the day and gaze at the drifting stars alongside the blue pine trees at night. The Grand Canyon, even within its deep gorge, provides a clear sky, making it an ideal place for stargazing.

Horseshoe Bend: Imprint of a Horseshoe

The Horseshoe Bend, shaped like a horseshoe print, features a large cliff in the center, surrounded by a river. It presents a unique and picturesque landscape. Be cautious of the narrow paths, and keep in mind the risk of falling due to the high terrain.

Antelope Canyon: Nature's Mystery

Antelope Canyon is a daytime attraction located within the Navajo Nation's Antelope Canyon Navajo Tribal Park. You must reserve a guided tour with the Navajo people to explore this canyon. The unique scenes, famously featured in Windows wallpapers, unfold as you traverse the narrow paths.

4 AM, the Darkest Hour

For the best stargazing experience at the Grand Canyon, the darkest hour is around 4 AM. While Joshua Tree focuses on observing stars during the day, at the Grand Canyon, it's more common to enjoy the stars first and savor the daylight later.

  • Experience the astronomical wonders and tranquility of nature on this beautiful journey. Unravel the secrets of the stars, and let this trip create unforgettable moments for you.
  • Embark on a starlit adventure, and immerse yourself in the mesmerizing mysteries of the universe!
